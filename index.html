ABOUT OUR SHOP

Welcome to Dapper Drip Coffeehouse, where sophistication meets the perfect brew. Nestled in the heart of the community, we’re more than just a coffee shop—we’re a space to gather, connect, and savor life’s simple joys. Our passion for coffee is matched only by our commitment to creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. From our meticulously sourced beans to our expertly crafted drinks, every cup tells a story of quality, care, and style. Whether you’re here for your morning pick-me-up, an afternoon escape, or an evening unwind, we have something special for you.

OUR SPECIALITIES

MOCHA

A shot of espresso is combined with chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream.

CORTADO

An espresso coffee drink, cut with a small amount of steamed milk.

FRAPPE

A Greek iced coffee drink generally made from spray-dried instant coffee, water, sugar, and milk.

LATTE

Espresso coffee drink topped with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk.

IRISH COFFEE

A caffeinated alcoholic drink consisting of Irish whiskey, hot coffee and sugar

ICED COFFEE

A cold version of your favourite coffee.
